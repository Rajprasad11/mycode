We at Emage Storr offer Image Consultation to Individuals on one-on-one bases. We address the ABC's of Image - Appearance, Behavior, and Communications skills - to help the individual develop a Polished Personal Style.

For Corporate clients, we offer Seminars/Workshops to team members to align their aspirations, ambitions, and personal goals with the Vision and Objective of the corporation. We ensure that participants adopt a presence and style that is in line with the Brand of the organization.



Neita Sehgal, Founder and Director of Emage Storr, created her own company to help people discover their potential, acknowledge their assets, do their best and be in charge of themselves.

With her extensive experience in Make up, Beauty, Fashion and peoples training, she went on to design individual image consultations and corporate seminars in Personal and Corporate Branding to empower, equip, and enhance people to have an advantage and be forerunners.

Neita is a Certified Image Consultant from Imageasia Training, an advanced image and professional development collaborations between ImageWorks Asia & London Image Institute. Her background includes Make up and Colour analysis for Chinese, Indian, and Caucasian skin types, a certification in Make up and Beauty Therapy from C.I.D.E.S.C.O. a diploma in Interior Design and Decoration, a diploma in Gemology (she designs and manufactures her own line of jewelry), over four years of experience as a bridal consultant, and numerous certifications in styling and makeup.

Her most recent works include:

  • Make up for Indian Woman’s Association Fashion Show – 'An Indian Affair'
  • Make up for runway models at the prestigious Indian designer Tarun Tahiliani's fashion show in Singapore
  • Working as a stylist, makeup artist, and fashion consultant for the Miss Malaysia and Ms. Malaysia beauty pageants
  • Developing and conducting staff Marketing and Training programs for The New Delhi Hilton and properties of the Taj Mahal Groups of Hotels in the areas of Grooming, Personality Development, Communication Skills, Operations, Processes, Branding, and Global Insight into Hospitality.
